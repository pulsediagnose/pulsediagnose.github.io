How does Pulse work?
 

1

Your team can connect easily

Connect to pulse via your web browser/phone/tablet. Tested with Windows, Mac, Linux on Chrome, Firefox, Brave and Edge. Connect directly from your site's fixed IP address, or via a secure VPN from home.

2

Intuitive Design

You don't have time to learn a fiddly system with 100s of clicks to do anything, it should be simple and obvious. Our system is designed a simple flow: Find the patient, see the history in a clear form, write your entry, offer treatment (prescribe, special authority, letter), and Save (it is also autosaved along the way).

3

Calculators and Tasks

Automate tasks and set reminders. Built in medical calculators and scoring sytsems. Need to easily do some obscure ADHD scoring system? Let me know and we can easily add it.

Consult Done
